1 Getting started

Open it

Open the link you were given in Safari (iPhone/iPad) or Chrome (Android/desktop). The app works entirely in your browser — nothing to install.

Add to your home screen (recommended)

  • iPhone / iPad: tap the Share button ⎙ in Safari, then choose Add to Home Screen. It will appear as an icon just like a regular app.
  • Android: tap the three-dot menu in Chrome, then Add to Home screen.
  • Desktop: bookmark it, or pin the tab.
Your data is saved automatically in your browser. It stays there between visits — even if you close the app.

2 Calendar

Tap calendar in the bottom bar. Use day week month in the top-right to switch views.

Add an event (day & week views)

  • Tap a day cell to open an input, type the event name, press Return.
  • In the week view, tap + under any day to jump to the day view and add there.

Add a multi-day span (month view)

  • Click and drag across several days — a label prompt appears when you release. Type the name (e.g. "Holiday") and press Return.
  • The span appears as a coloured bar across those days.

Edit or delete

  • Edit event: double-tap the event text to rename it inline.
  • Delete event: hover (or tap) to reveal the × button.
  • Delete span: hover the span bar to reveal ×; double-click the label to rename it.
Navigating months: use ← prev and next → buttons just below the header.

3 To-do list

Tap to-do in the bottom bar.

Add an item

  • Type in the box at the bottom and press Return or tap add.

Tick something off

  • Tap the square checkbox to the left of any item. It turns filled when done, and the text gets a strikethrough.
  • The progress bar at the top shows how many items are complete.

Rename an item

  • Double-tap the item text to edit it inline. Press Return or tap away to save.

Reorder items

  • Grab the ⠿ handle on the left of any row and drag it up or down.
  • Works with touch on mobile too — press and hold the handle, then drag.

Delete an item

  • Tap (or hover on desktop) to reveal the × button on the right, then tap it.

Section headers

  • You can add headings to group your tasks. Type something like "This week" or "Someday" and it'll be added as a plain task first — to convert it to a section heading, just drag it above other items to use as a divider. (Section headers are items with the header flag — double-tap to rename them.)
Everything saves automatically as you go. No save button needed.

4 Common questions

Will my data sync across my devices?

Not automatically — each browser keeps its own copy. For now, the app is best used on one device (e.g. always your phone). Sync across devices may come later.

What if I clear my browser data?

Your calendar and to-do items are stored in your browser's local storage. Clearing site data will erase them, so avoid doing that if you want to keep your entries.

Can anyone else see my list?

No. Your data never leaves your device — it's not sent to any server.

The app looks odd / things are missing

Try a hard refresh: hold Shift and tap the reload button, or close and reopen the tab.
